**Meeting Title**: Distrubted Inference Network (DIN)  and Internet of Agent (IOA)

**Meeting Description**: 

The explosive growth in AI large model inference services marks the arrival of the accessible AI era,  presenting networking with challenges and opportunities. Centralized deployment inference architectures struggle to scale rapidly enough for the demands of massive concurrent users and exploding application growth. It is needed to research new distributed inference network (DIN) architectures, scenes and technologies to address the challenge of support huge numbers of simultaneous users. Moreover, as AI inference evolves beyond human-AI text/multimedia interaction towards GenAI, and human-AI Agent and Agent-to-Agent interactions, potentially forming an Internet of Agents (IOA). DIN may serve as a foundational layer for future IOA architectures.

Based on the above understanding, we will organize a side meeting at Segovia room on Wednesday, July 23, 9:30-11:00 AM , hoping to reach some consensus on the problems and related requirements, and these emerging possibilities, inviting insights on how DIN can support the evolving landscape of agent interactions and IOA.
